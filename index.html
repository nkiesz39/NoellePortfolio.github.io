Trends in Skill Requirements for Developers

In this project, I assumed the role as a Data Analyst at a Global IT and Business Consulting firm. Leveraging data from StackOverflow's Annual Developer Survey obtained through web scraping and API calls, along with a Kaggle dataset, I analyzed patterns and trends of technologies most utilized by developers and those they would like to learn in the future. The objective was to identify areas for equipping team and hiring managers with insights to facilitate upskilling initiatives, ensuring their employees remain competitive in the market. The deliverables provided actionable insights, interactive dashboards to utilize for presenting the findings, and a PowerPoint presentation. This project was used as a part of the IBM Data Analyst Certification Capstone.
